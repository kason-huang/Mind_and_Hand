> Overview of the HumanNet data pipeline. The pipeline is organized into three stages. (1) Data Collection couples keyword discovery, including seed keywords, keyword expansion, keyword-based crawling, channel crawling, and existing sources, with content search and retrieval over video platforms, general search engines, open-source datasets, and self-collection under real-world environments, yielding a unified pool of mixed videos. (2) Data Processing converts raw videos into clip-level samples through de-duplication and normalization, content filtering, quality filtering, scene splitting by visual change, and video clipping. (3) Annotation enriches the processed clips with 3D hand and body pose detection, monocular SLAM, motion retargeting, and LLM-assisted captioning that produces video captions, motion descriptions, and activity classifications, resulting in a large-scale human-centric dataset with diverse scenes and robot-ready subsets.

> 数据集的来源决定了质量分层
> HumanNet 的数据来自全网公开视频，不是实验室拍的 “完美素材”：
> 很多用户上传的视频，本身就有遮挡、模糊、视角问题，所以姿态检测分数低。
> 也有很多清晰的教学视频、自拍视频，姿态完整清晰，所以分数高。
> 过滤策略的影响
> 论文的过滤不是 “一刀切” 把低分样本全删掉，而是保留了大部分样本：
> 删掉的是分数极低（比如 0 分附近）的无效视频。
> 但 0.2~0.3 分的样本，虽然检测置信度低，但视频内容还是有效的，所以被保留了下来。
> ^429FTSKAaTKJHZJS3p8

> 这正是 HumanNet 的聪明之处：
> 高分样本（右峰）：提供干净、可靠的姿态先验，让模型学会标准的动作。
> 低分样本（左峰）：提供真实、带噪声的场景数据，让模型学会在遮挡、模糊的情况下也能识别动作，变得更鲁棒。
> 两者结合，模型才能学到既标准、又能适应真实世界的动作表示，而不是只会处理实验室里的 “完美视频”。
