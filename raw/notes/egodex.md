## Abstract

EgoDex introduces large-scale egocentric video collected with Apple Vision Pro for dexterous manipulation learning. 829 hours, 194 tasks, 338K trajectories, 25-joint 3D hand skeleton tracking.

## Key Contributions

- Apple Vision Pro data collection pipeline (ARKit)
- 829 hours, 90M frames, 338K trajectories
- 25 joints/hand 3D skeleton + natural language annotations
- Largest dexterous human manipulation dataset
- Used by EgoScale (Stage I) and Being-H0 (UniHand)
