## Abstract

HumanNet: Scaling Human-centric Video Learning to One Million Hours. Proposes a large-scale human-centric video dataset and pipeline for robot learning.

## Key Contributions

- 1 million hours of human-centric video data
- Validation that 1,000h human video ≈ 100h robot data
- Three-stage data pipeline: collection, processing, annotation
- Large-scale scaling law verification
